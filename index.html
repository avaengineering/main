Our Manufacturing Process

Defined procedures for mould design, tooling, sampling, and production with traceable outputs

Design

Mould Design & Engineering

CAD-driven design review and mould flow analysis to optimize part quality and production efficiency.

  • 3D CAD modelling (SolidWorks, CATIA)
  • Mould flow simulation and gate analysis
  • GD&T verification and tolerance review
  • Parting line and draft angle optimization
  • Design for manufacturability (DFM) feedback

Tooling

Tool Manufacturing

Precision machining and fabrication of mould components using CNC and EDM processes.

  • CNC milling for cavity and core machining
  • EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining)
  • Steel hardening and heat treatment
  • Cooling channel drilling and layout
  • Assembly, fitment, and alignment checks

Injection

Injection Moulding Production

High-precision injection moulding with process control and parameter optimization for consistent output.

  • Material drying and conditioning
  • Machine setup and parameter settings
  • Shot weight and cycle time optimization
  • First article inspection (FAI)
  • Continuous process monitoring

QC

Quality Control & Inspection

Dimensional verification and visual inspection based on OEM specifications and control plans.

  • CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine)
  • Visual and functional testing
  • Critical dimension verification
  • PPAP documentation and reporting
  • Non-conformance tracking and CAPA

Delivery

Packaging & Dispatch

Secure packaging and logistics coordination for on-time delivery to customer locations across India.

  • Industrial packaging and labelling
  • Dispatch documentation and invoicing
  • Logistics partner coordination
  • Tracking and delivery confirmation
  • After-sales support and feedback
